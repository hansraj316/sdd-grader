# Feature Specification: Reporting

## User Scenarios & Testing

### User Story 1 - View reports (Priority: P1)

As an analyst, I want to view reports.

**Acceptance criteria**

- Given data exists, when I open reports, then I see a summary table.

### Edge Cases

- An empty dataset shows an empty-state message.

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

- FR-001: The system shall have low latency and high availability.
- FR-002: The reporting service shall be highly scalable.

## Success Criteria

### Measurable Outcomes

- Reports should feel responsive.
